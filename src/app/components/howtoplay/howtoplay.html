How to Play

01

Choose Mode

Select your favorite card content: Abstract Icons, Numbers, or Neon Emojis. Each requires a different cognitive focus.

02

Select Density

Pick a challenge level. Beginner is great for warmups, while Expert will test the limits of your kinetic memory.

03

Find Pairs

Flip two cards at a time. If they match, they stay up. If not, they'll flip back after a second. Remember their positions!

04

Beat the Clock

Try to complete the grid in the fewest moves and the shortest time possible to improve your Global Memory IQ.

⚡ Pro Tip: Focus on patterns rather than individual cards to process dense grids faster.
